Week 6 • Day 3 • Dropped H — Function Words in Connected Speech

صوت الـ /h/ بيفضل كامل لما: (١) الكلمة تبدأ الجملة، (٢) الكلمة عليها نبر للتوضيح، (٣) have/has/had مستخدمة كفعل أساسي بمعنى 'يملك'، مش كمساعد.

🔊 Target Sounds / الأصوات المستهدفة: contrast: when /h/ IS pronounced (sentence-start, stress, main verb)

📝 Minimal Pairs / أزواج التمييز

Tell him. (dropped H, mid-sentence) / Him? Never. (full H, sentence-start)
I have been there. (dropped H, auxiliary) / I have a car. (full H, main verb = own)

🎯 Practice Words / كلمات للتمرين

Him • Her • I have a car • I have been there • HER, not him

🎙️ Say This / قول ده

"Him? He never called her. I have a car, but I have been walking lately — and I told HER the news, not him."
