Week 6 • Day 2 • Dropped H — Function Words in Connected Speech

في الكلام السريع بدون نبر، أفعال المساعدة have و has و had (لما تستخدم كأفعال مساعدة، مش كأفعال أساسية) غالبًا بتخسر صوت الـ /h/، خصوصًا بعد ضمير.

🔊 Target Sounds / الأصوات المستهدفة: dropped /h/ in auxiliary verbs: have, has, had

📝 Minimal Pairs / أزواج التمييز

I have been (h dropped, natural) / I. Have. Been. (h kept, careful)
she has gone (h dropped) / she. Has. Gone. (h kept)

🎯 Practice Words / كلمات للتمرين

I have been • she has gone • we had left • you have seen • he has told • they had known

🎙️ Say This / قول ده

"I have been waiting since she has gone — we had left before you have seen it, and he has told them what they had known."
